A Poem For Fiona

Posted on 2017-02-06 Edited on 2020-03-27 In Bob , 杂谈

Poem of Tecumseh

Live your life that fear of death can never enter your heat.

_Trouble no one about his religion. _

Respect others and their views and demand they respect yours.

_ Love your life , perfect your life_

Beautify all things in your life

Seek to make your life long and of service to your people

When your time comes to die

Be not like those whose hearts are filled with fear of death.

So that when their time comes they weep and pray

For a little more time to live their lives over again in different way

Sing your death song and die like a hero going home
